Hi hi!

I’m Renz — a curious, hands-on, and slightly all-over-the-place kind of tech enthusiast (in the best way possible).

Back in university at PUP Quezon City, I started out with a passion for design — UI/UX caught my eye first. But that was just the beginning. I eventually found myself diving into frontend, backend, and everything in between — which led me to become a full-stack developer...

Not long after,

I tried on the hat of a Project Manager, leading teams, writing documentation, and keeping things on track. Then came Quality Assurance, where I discovered the thrill of catching bugs, writing test cases, and ensuring things actually work the way they’re supposed to. I even explored cybersecurity, competing in capture-the-flag challenges and learning tools that opened up a whole new world.

You could say I’ve been everywhere — and I mean that in the best way. Each role taught me something new, but one stood out the most:

Quality Assurance

While often seen as the quiet, behind-the-scenes role, I see QA as the glue that holds great projects together. Testing, documentation, debugging — it all matters. And I genuinely enjoy doing it.

Welcome to my portfolio — a little corner of the web that shows where I’ve been, what I’ve built, and what I love doing.
